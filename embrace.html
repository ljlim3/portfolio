EMBRACE

Parent Dashboard

What's EMBRACE?


It's an intelligent tutoring application that aims to foster parent-child dialogic reading and, in turn, the reading comprenhension of the child dual-language learners.

Why parent dashboard?


The EMBRACE parent dashboard serves primarily as a personal informatics tool to help parents understand their reading habits and question-asking behaviors, and show whether or not they are fulfilling the app's requirements.
